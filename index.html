Smart Irrigation using Arduino

The **Smart Irrigation System using Arduino** is an automated solution designed to improve water efficiency in agriculture and gardening. It uses soil moisture sensors to monitor the water content in the soil and automatically operates a water pump or valve when the moisture level falls below a predefined threshold. This ensures that plants receive the right amount of water at the right time, reducing both water wastage and manual effort. The system can be enhanced with additional sensors such as temperature, humidity, or rain detectors for more accurate control, and can also be integrated with GSM, Wi-Fi, or Bluetooth modules for remote monitoring and control. Being low-cost, energy-efficient, and sustainable, this project is highly suitable for small-scale farmers, greenhouses, and home gardens, ultimately promoting smart agriculture and better resource management.
